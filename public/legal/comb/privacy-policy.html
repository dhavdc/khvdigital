Privacy Policy for Comb

Effective Date: January 3, 2026

KHV Digital LLC ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Comb mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use Comb.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Photos: When you use Comb to preview hairstyles, you provide photos of yourself. These photos are processed by our AI system but are NOT permanently stored on our servers.
  • Preferences: Your gender preference and hairstyle/color selections to personalize your experience.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Identifier: A randomly generated identifier stored on your device, used solely for rate limiting (tracking daily generation limits for free users).
  • Usage Data: Basic usage information such as hairstyle selections and generation requests to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Generate Hairstyle Previews: Your photos are sent to our AI processing system to create hairstyle previews. Photos are processed in real-time and are NOT stored after processing is complete.
  • Personalization: Your gender preference is used to show relevant hairstyle options.
  • Rate Limiting: Your device identifier is used to enforce daily generation limits for free users (3 per day).
  • Service Improvement: Aggregated, anonymized usage data helps us improve the App.

3. Data Retention

  • Original Photos: NOT stored. Photos are processed in memory and discarded immediately after AI processing.
  • Generated Preview Images: Stored temporarily for up to 7 days to allow you to download and share your results, then automatically deleted.
  • Rate Limiting Data: Device identifiers and daily counts are retained for 25 hours, then automatically deleted.
  • Processing Records: Job metadata is retained for 1-24 hours for debugging purposes, then automatically deleted.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate Comb:

  • Google Gemini AI: Powers our hairstyle generation. Your photos are sent to Google's AI system for processing. Google's privacy policy applies to this processing. Google Privacy Policy
  • Railway: Provides our hosting infrastructure, including temporary image storage and job queue management.
  • RevenueCat: Manages premium subscriptions and purchases. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: Error tracking to help us identify and fix issues. Sentry Privacy Policy

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • Generated images are stored with secure, time-limited access URLs
  • No user accounts or passwords are required, minimizing data exposure
  • Automatic data deletion ensures minimal data retention

6. Your Rights

Since we do not maintain user accounts and retain minimal data:

  • You can cancel any in-progress generation to immediately delete associated data
  • Generated images automatically expire after 7 days
  • You can uninstall the App at any time to stop all data collection
  • For data deletion requests, contact us at support@khvdigital.com

7. Children's Privacy

Comb is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" above.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
